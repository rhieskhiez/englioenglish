Frequently Asked Questions

What distinguishes The Speakers' Room?

The Speakers' Room is our complimentary weekly forum where participants engage in structured conversation practice. Limited to 14 participants, these sessions provide intensive speaking opportunities in a professional yet supportive environment.

Do you offer remote learning options?

Indeed. Our hybrid learning model allows participants to choose between in-person sessions at our Batam Centre facility or premium online instruction via our dedicated platform. Both formats maintain our exceptional educational standards.

What proficiency levels do you accommodate?

We cater to all proficiency levels from elementary to advanced. Our comprehensive assessment process ensures optimal class placement, maximising learning efficiency and participant satisfaction.

What is your class size policy?

Standard classes are limited to 8 participants, ensuring personalised attention and maximum speaking opportunities. We also offer exclusive one-to-one tuition for accelerated progress.

Where are you located?

Our primary facility is located at KDA Junction, Block C4 E, Batam Centre. The Speakers' Room convenes at Elora Coffee, Batam Centre—both venues are easily accessible with ample parking.
