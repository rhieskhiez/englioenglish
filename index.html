Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Speakers' Room?

The Speakers' Room is our FREE weekly speaking practice session held every Saturday. It's a fun, judgment-free zone where you can practice real-life English conversations with other learners. Limited to 14 participants for quality interaction!

Do you offer online classes?

Yes! We offer hybrid learning - you can choose between offline classes at our Batam Center location or online classes via Zoom. Both options provide the same quality education with interactive sessions.

What levels do you teach?

We teach all levels from beginner to advanced. Before starting, we'll assess your current level to place you in the right class. We have General English, Business English, and Conversation classes for different needs.

How many students per class?

Our regular classes have a maximum of 8 students to ensure everyone gets personal attention. We also offer 1-on-1 private sessions for those who prefer individual learning.

Where is your location?

We're located at KDA Junction, Block C4 E, Batam Center. For The Speakers' Room sessions, we meet at Elora Coffee, Batam Center. Easy to find and accessible!
